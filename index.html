The Auditorium

The auditorium at Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Technology (GIKI) is a contemporary space that accommodates a substantial audience for academic lectures, seminars, and cultural events. With its sleek design and optimal seating arrangement, the auditorium ensures a comfortable and engaging experience for attendees. Equipped with advanced audio-visual technology, it serves as a versatile venue for presentations and performances. Beyond its academic role, the auditorium is a cultural hub, fostering artistic expression and community engagement. Its strategic location within the institute enhances accessibility, and the facility is designed to accommodate diverse needs, including those of differently-abled individuals. For the latest and most accurate information, it is advisable to refer to the institute's official publications or contact relevant authorities.
